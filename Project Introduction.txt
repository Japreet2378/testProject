My project is on creating a smoothie ordering app called"Jugo Juice".
This app will allow the customers to order beforehand and can pick 
their smoothies or juices at the nearby store.This app will be selling
juices ,smoothhies,breakfast wraps and sandwiches  and other stuff like
water bottles,chocolate bars.Moreover, customers can customize their 
smoothies as per their requirement.Apart from this, nutrition guide will
be provided to the customers.Also,Users will be able to view the page in
their own preffered language.
